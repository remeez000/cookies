Delicious Chocloate Chip Cookies

Preparation time:30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instruction

  1. preheat oven to 350 degreeF(175 Degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In separate bowl, combine the flour and baking soda; gradually add to the butter mixture.
  5. stir in the chocolate chops.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
